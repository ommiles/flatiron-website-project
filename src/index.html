A bit about me...

  1. (01)

    Texas Roots

    Earlier this year, I began tracing my family's roots back to the 1800s and discovered I'm the descendant of a strong line of Texan farmers.  My grandmother, her mother, and her mother were all raised on shared farms in a small black community in Brazos and Van Alstyne.

    My family has come to Califormia via the Great Migration.  You can learn more about that HERE

  2. (02)

    Dance Like No One's Watching

    I attended a conservatory high-school in Los Angeles called LACHSA and also went on to major in Dance Composition at the University of the Arts in Philadelphia.  To this day, although I no longer dance professionally, I try to stay as physical as posible with cycling, hip-hop dance class and hiking.

  3. (03)

    Always Overcaffeinated

    My first paying jobs after college were at coffee business.  Shortly after, I decided to dedicate myself to the growing industry of Specialty Coffee.  I worked at pivotal moments at companies like Blue Bottle Coffee and Allegro Coffee; moments of exciting M&A's with Nestle and Amazon, international expansion.

    Through my roles in specialty coffee over the past 10 years, I've liked in Oakland, New York, Denver, and Los Angeles.  I've also grown my skills to consulting, public speaking engagements, and live instruction.  This has enabled me to travel to places like Budapest, Amsterdam, Lisbon, Reykjavik, London, and Barcelona.

    Because of my background in coffee and my love for it, expect me to be characteristically over caffeinated. :)
